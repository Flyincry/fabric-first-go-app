Jewelry Supply Chain Financing Platform

In order to solve the financing difficulties of the traditional jewelry industry, Center of Blockchain and Intelligence Technology (CBIT) has developed a jewelry supply chain financing platform based on blockchain data management system. The platform makes use of the characteristics of blockchain, such as tamper proof, easy certificate storage and high performance, and provides integrated data services for suppliers, financiers and other upstream and downstream enterprises.
